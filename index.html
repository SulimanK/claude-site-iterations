I'm a Lead UX Consultant with eight years experience designing across GOV.UK One Login, NHS login, the Department for Education, TalkTalk, and beyond.

Half a million people had created accounts through the authentication flow I designed for GOV.UK One Login, and the number will be considerably higher now. More lastingly, the patterns I contributed, including "confirm your phone number", are now part of the GOV.UK Design System.

Outside of client work, I support communities of practice, build documentation standards that teams can actually sustain, and I'm actively exploring what agentic delivery looks like in practice, bringing the wider team along with me.

I lead by creating the conditions for good work: building trust with stakeholders and engineers, mentoring designers, and giving people the space to do their best thinking.
